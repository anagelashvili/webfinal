Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation directly through our website by searching for hotels on the Hotels page, selecting your desired dates, and completing the booking process. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team for assistance.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by hotel. Most hotels allow free cancellation up to 24-48 hours before check-in. Please check the specific cancellation policy for your reservation during the booking process or in your confirmation email.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for group bookings of 10 rooms or more. Please contact our group reservations department for more information and personalized service for your group travel needs.

How can I modify my reservation?

You can modify your reservation by logging into your account on our website and accessing your booking details. For complex changes or if you booked through a third party, please contact our customer service team for assistance.
